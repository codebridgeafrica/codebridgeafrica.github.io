The Big Picture

Africa Needs More Tech Graduates

Africa's digital economy is growing at an extraordinary pace — but the continent still faces a critical shortage of qualified technology professionals. Universities alone cannot close this gap fast enough.

CodeBridge Africa was founded to be part of the solution: starting with accessible, quality short-form education and building systematically toward a fully accredited higher education institution.

Every certificate earned, every diploma awarded, every business served by our IT team brings us one step closer to that vision.

The Journey in Detail

Current Phase

Phase 1: Certificates & Diplomas

CodeBridge Africa is actively operating as a coding school and IT services provider. We offer short-form certificates and diplomas recognised by partner employers.

  • Kids, Teen & Adult programmes running
  • 6–24 week certificate courses
  • IT services division operational
  • GitHub Pages website live
  • Student management system in development
2
In Progress

Phase 2: Advanced Diplomas & Partnerships

Formalising our curriculum framework, pursuing partnerships with established universities for credit transfer and recognition.

  • Curriculum alignment to NaCCA / GES standards
  • University partnership MOUs
  • Expanded campus and online infrastructure
  • Instructor professional development programme
  • First cohort of Advanced Diploma graduates
3
Planned

Phase 3: Grow-in Accredited Diploma

Applying for formal accreditation as a technical and vocational education institution. Our diplomas will carry national recognition.

  • TVET registration & certification (Ghana)
  • Nationally accredited diploma programmes
  • Credit hours system for degree pathways
  • Research & publication programme initiated
4
Vision

Phase 4: Accredited CS Degrees

The ultimate milestone — becoming a fully accredited degree-awarding institution for Computer Science across Africa.

  • BSc Computer Science (3-year programme)
  • BSc Software Engineering
  • BSc Data Science & Artificial Intelligence
  • Postgraduate pathways (MSc)
  • Multi-campus presence across West Africa
  • International accreditation & exchange programmes
