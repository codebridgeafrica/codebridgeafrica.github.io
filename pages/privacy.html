Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and course interests when you register as a student or contact us through our website forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to: process your enrolment application, communicate with you about your courses, send programme updates, and improve our services.

3. Email Communications

By submitting a registration or contact form, you consent to receiving email communications from CodeBridge Africa. You may opt out at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses Formspree (formspree.io) to process form submissions. Form data is transmitted securely and is subject to Formspree's own privacy policy. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

For students under 18, we require parental/guardian consent for enrolment. Parents or guardians may contact us to review, update, or delete their child's information.

7. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at hello@codebridgeafrica.com.
