About CodeBridge Africa

Our mission, vision, values, and the team behind the bridge.

Our Mission

Bridging the Digital Divide, One Student at a Time

CodeBridge Africa exists to make high-quality Computer Science education accessible to every African, regardless of background, age, or prior experience. We believe that coding is the new literacy, and that Africa's future depends on building a generation of confident, creative technology builders.

From a child writing their first line of Scratch code in Accra, to a professional earning an accredited Computer Science degree, we provide the pathway.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading accredited Computer Science university — built from the ground up, for Africans, by Africans.

Our Method

Curriculum-aligned, project-based, and industry-connected learning from day one.
